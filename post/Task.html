About TaskMate

TaskMate is an intuitive solution for managing tasks and meetings within an organization. From creating meetings to assigning tasks and setting agendas, TaskMate helps streamline workflows, boost productivity, and improve team collaboration. It's the ultimate tool for staying organized and on track.

Features

Meeting Scheduler

Easily schedule meetings and invite team members with integrated calendar functionality.

Task Assignment

Assign tasks to team members, set deadlines, and monitor progress in real-time.

Agenda Management

Plan and share detailed agendas to ensure meetings stay focused and productive.

Notification System

Stay updated with instant notifications for task updates and meeting reminders.

Analytics and Reports

Generate detailed insights on task completion rates, meeting efficiency, and more.

Collaboration Tools

Enable team members to share files, leave comments, and collaborate seamlessly.
